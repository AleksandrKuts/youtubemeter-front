Youtube channel analytics: likes, dizlik cheating, twisting views etc

It is used to analyze the work of youtube channels, tracks statistics on videos: likes, dislikes, views, comments. This allows you to record an anomalous change in these characteristics, for example:

  • Twisting video views: BOEHHO-БACKETБOЛЬHЫE COЛДATЫ
  • Detailed twisting video views: BOEHHO-БACKETБOЛЬHЫE COЛДATЫ
  • Cheat dislikes video: В Кремле паника: Анаконда на границе с РФ. У Украины теперь есть ударные БПЛА.
  • Cheat dislikes video: Е. Понасенков: тайна Возрождения, задания в посольстве, трагедия в Керчи, Вермахт и 1941 год

The collection of metrics is only for video from channels, the list of which is managed by the administrator - he is the author of the program. The following metrics are collected:

  • likes
  • dislikes
  • views
  • comments

The service is used to collect metrics: Youtube API

The period for which metrics are collected is two weeks from the moment the video was published, longer to collect metrics does not make sense. Metrics are collected once per minute.

The service periodically, once every three minutes, checks channels for the appearance of new videos, and automatically adds them to processing.

The collection of metrics starts from the moment an administrator adds a channel, so the metrics data for older videos may be missing or incomplete (only from the date of upload).

The list of channels does not fully reflect the preferences of the author, some are added as a good object for research. Also on the list are neutral non-political channels as control group.

The service is written with the aim of objectively studying the effect of bots on YouTube, primarily to understand this phenomenon by the author.
